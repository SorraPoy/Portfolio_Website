A Little Bit About Me

My journey into game development began when I was 14 years old. At that time, I hadn't found any particular career passion. Although I had been an gamer since childhood. It was during this period that games like "Undertale" and "Stardew Valley" were released. After playing and studying these games, I discovered that they were each developed by a single person. This revelation became my main inspiration to pursue and study game development. Driven by the dream of someday creating something remarkable like they did.
Fast forward to the present, after pursuing my desired education. I've explored various fields including compositing, 3D modeling, web development, and ultimately, game development. My experience encompasses the entire game development process - from concept design and storyline planning to organizing various components, all the way through refinement and completion. This driving force has led me to deeply love and enjoy game development.
When I'm not in front of a computer screen. I enjoy running in public parks, studying history and legends, and experimenting with new melodies on my piano.
